No money to open a shop? No problem, its free!

When beginning a business, start-ups often do not have money to pay for a web application. Can small businesses afford such a risky venture? All of this using the secure currency of bitcoins!

Not only that, with our "Streets" view, "Top Shop" Window, and advertised sales, your shop has all it needs to be discovered and succeed for little to no cost.
